## Brand Colors (One Group Overrides)

| Token Role | Name | Hex | CSS Variable | Usage |
|------------|------|-----|-------------|-------|
| Brand Accent | One Group Red | `#762224` | `--primary` | Primary CTAs, brand moments |
| Accent Light | Bright Crimson | `#c45a5c` | `--accent` | Links on dark, hover, secondary emphasis |
| Page Canvas | Warm Cream | `#f5f3ed` | `--background` | Primary light background |
| Card Surface | Pure White | `#ffffff` | `--card` | Cards, elevated containers |
| Dark Surface | Charcoal | `#323234` | `--card` (dark) | Dark-theme containers, nav |
| Deep Dark | Cool Deep Black | `#121214` | `--background` (dark) | Dark-theme page background |
| Error | Bright Alert Red | `#d4453a` | `--destructive` | Error states — NOT brand red |
